Family Law

The team at John O’Leary Solicitors has extensive experience advising clients on a wide range of family law matters, providing clear and compassionate legal guidance during what can often be a difficult and emotional time.

We understand that family law issues are highly personal and can have long-lasting effects on individuals and families. Our approach is focused on listening carefully to our clients, understanding their circumstances, and delivering practical advice tailored to their specific needs.

Our firm provides assistance at all stages of family law proceedings, including matters relating to children such as custody, access, guardianship, and maintenance. We also advise clients in relation to domestic violence issues, including applications for safety, protection, and barring orders, offering sensitive and supportive representation throughout the process.

John O’Leary Solicitors has significant experience in separation and divorce cases, including judicial separation, divorce applications, and the negotiation of separation agreements. We regularly advise on the division of assets, property and pension adjustment orders, and financial settlements, ensuring that our clients’ rights and interests are protected.

Where possible, we seek to resolve family law matters through negotiation and agreement, helping to minimise conflict and legal costs. However, when court proceedings are necessary, we provide strong and effective representation in the District and Circuit Courts.

Our aim is to deliver a comprehensive and cost-effective family law service, while maintaining open communication and a supportive, professional approach at all times.

For further information or to arrange a consultation, please contact John O’Leary Solicitors – Family Law Department.
